Guillaume Staerman

PhD Student in Machine Learning at Télécom Paris


About me

I am a PhD student at Télécom Paris (Institut Polytechnique de Paris) in the Team Signal, Statistique et Apprentissage (S2A) since January 2019. My advisors are Pavlo Mozharovskyi, Stéphan Clémençon and Florence d'Alché-Buc.


Research interests

My research interest lies at the boundary between machine learning, statistics and signal processing. My work focuses on anomaly detection for functional data, i.e., when data belongs to functional spaces.
